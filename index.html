COVID-19 Lockdown has been emotionally stressful for some people, while for others, it has been an impromptu time-out from their hectic routines, hence helping them rediscover their hobbies at their leisure! While it is okay, however you have spent your quarantine, in your own way, do you want to know how YOU have been, compared to others, during this lockdown? Were you too stressed? Were you careless? Or did you just do fine?

Let's Find Out !

Disclaimer: This survey assumes you have had certain privileges during this quarantine, like staying at home and being able to spend most of the time with family or doing your own favourite things alone, not in much distress. We know a lot of people were not this privileged, and we are greatly inspired and extend our huge support to them.
